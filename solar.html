SOLAR

This project lasts for two years. It contains 5 field studies and has later become the topic we presented at 2018 UN Climate Change Conference, Poland.
This project contains three stages:(1) Several field studies for investigation into Chinese PV Town;(2) Life Cycle Assessment for the evaluation of environmental impact; (3) Presentation at COP24, Poland.

Motivation

The solar project was started from a new concept "Energy Interconnection". For the increasing ratio of renewable energy in electricity, a network of distributed generation system is needed. To understand the future of energy interconnection we did serial studies solar energy distributed system (One of the most common renewable energy) in China.

Electricity Usage Pattern

Evaluating the workload of electricity is important for the overall assessment of efficiency in distributed energy system. Thus we concluded household electricity usage pattern. The data was derived from 324 users.



Statistical Results

We did life circle assessment of Greenhouse Gas Emissions Solar Photovoltaics. Summary of results of the systematic review and harmonization of estimates of life cycle GHG emissions for selected electricity generation technologies, with a focus on PV technologies.
