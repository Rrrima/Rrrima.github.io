MELODY FLOW

wThis project is based on ‘Magenta’ , an open-sourced tool for machine learning in the creative process, developed by Google Brain. We tend to use RNN to generate music in different genres.
In this project, we trained models with light music, popular music and classical music. The model we used including (1) traditional LSTM; (2) LSTM with LookBack mechanism; (3) LSTM with Attention mechanism. (You can find pieces of music we generated in the result section.

Motovation

Inspired by the idea of style transfer in images, we hope to extract features in different genres of music and generate music by using several given notes as primer. We choose Recurrent Neural Network (RNN) as the basis of our model, as is suitable for extracting features in time sequence.
